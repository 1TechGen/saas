A Guide to Writing Effective AI Image Prompts

Why Prompts Matter

AI models create images based on your text descriptions. The more precise and descriptive your prompt, the better the result. A vague prompt gives random results, while a well-written one can produce professional art. To see the tool in action, check out our AI Image Generator.

Key Elements of a Strong Prompt

  1. Subject: What is the main focus? (e.g., "a cyberpunk samurai")
  2. Style: What art style do you want? (e.g., "digital painting, anime style")
  3. Details: Add lighting, colors, or textures. (e.g., "neon lights, rainy streets")
  4. Composition: How should it be framed? (e.g., "close-up portrait, cinematic")

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too vague: Instead of "A beautiful landscape," try "A serene mountain lake at dawn with misty forests."
  • Overcomplicating: Avoid long, conflicting descriptions that can confuse the AI.
